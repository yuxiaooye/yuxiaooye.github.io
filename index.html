I am a first year PhD Student at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Ling Pan. Previously, I was a MSc Student at Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT), advised by Chi Harold Liu. I obtained my bachelor's degree from BIT in 2022.

My main research interest is in intelligent decision-making, include (Multi-Agent) Reinforcement Learning and LLM Agents (code generation, text-to-SQL). I have published papers in conferences related to AI and IoT such as ICDE, JSAC and INFOCOM.
